The future of sports is social

ATLETO is an app that connects everyday athletes.
Our goal is to create more and better sports experiences all over the world by connecting people who has a wish of being active and social at the same time.


Why Atleto? Because...


  • ... You always seem to be a player short when playing b-ball.
  • ... Your tennis partner has a habit of cancelling at the last minute.
  • ... Running alone just isn't as motivating
  • ... Organizing the weekly soccer match is a time-consuming hassle
  • ... You don't want to drive across town only to discover that no one showed up for the pick-up game in the park

ATLETO connects everyday athletes

1. Select your favourite sport

Start by creating your profile, choose your favorite sports and set your skill level .

2. Connect with friends

Connect with friends and strangers to build your Social Sports Network.

3. Join or Host games

Find nearby games based on your sport, skill level and mode.
